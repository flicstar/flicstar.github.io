I am a technical communicator working in (mostly) open source. I enjoy helping writers and non-writers create great written content! I work remote and asynchronous, and I'm based in Melbourne, Australia.

Day Job

I work as a technical editor and writer at Open Strategy Partners, a strategic communications agency. I love being a part of this team, where I can write and speak about writing and editing.

Open source

I am a member of the TYPO3 Association, an open source CMS. I contribute to that project in the Content Team. This is a great community. If you're looking for a CMS I can highly recommend it!

I helped found The Good Docs Project in 2019, creating best practice templates and writing instructions for documenting open source software. It's heaps cool over there - you should totally check it out.

Books

I helped update The Technical Writing Process book to the 2nd edition. My main contribution was about reviewing drafts, editing and giving feedback. check out chapter 22! I co-authored The TYPO3 Guidebook, published January 2021.
